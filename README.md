📌 Project Overview

This project investigates how household environmental conditions affect chronic diseases among India's elderly population using the Longitudinal Ageing Study in India (LASI) dataset.

Environmental determinants such as cooking fuel, sanitation, drinking water, and housing quality were analyzed to understand their association with diseases like:

❤️ Heart Disease

👁️ Cataract

🫁 Lung Disease

A Household Environment Quality Index (HEQI) was developed using Principal Component Analysis (PCA) to measure overall household environmental quality.

🎯 Objectives

Examine the relationship between household environment and elderly health outcomes

Construct a Household Environment Quality Index (HEQI)

Identify key environmental risk factors linked to chronic diseases

📊 Dataset
